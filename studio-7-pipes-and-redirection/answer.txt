6. (2) Advanatages

- Abstraction: Programs don't need to know the source/destination of their I/O they just use stdin/stdout. 
- Flexibility: The OS can redirect I/O to files, pipes, sockets, and more. Without modifying the programs, enabling powerful shell pipelines and IPC.
